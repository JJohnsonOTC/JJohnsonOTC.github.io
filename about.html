My Name is Jeff Johnson


I am originally from Southeast Kansas, where I spent most of my youth playing sports, farming, hunting and fishing. For the last 7 years, I have been living in Springfield, Missouri.


This has been a great place to call home, with plenty of great restaurants (especially Rama Thai!) as well as some great hiking trails and lakes to take advantage of.




For over 10 years I have been deploying, administering and hardening networks, firewalls, and systems - currently as a member of GrayGuard Technology.

I also train & manage staff, handle documentation, compliance evidence collection & submissions, penetration testing, risk assesments, and user training.

Skillset:

  • Programming & Development (C#, Python, HTML, CSS, PL/B)
  • Scripting (Powershell, Bash, Windows Shell)
  • Network, Firewall & IDS/IPS installation
  • Information Security Education (Social Engineering, etc.)
  • Compliance Documentation
  • Risk Assessments
  • Network hardening
  • Penetration Testing
  • Some Database administration (SQL, Access, DB2)
